## Networking Strategy

### Warm Connections (Highest Priority)

#### BAE Systems Network
1. **Former supervisor/mentor** → Request referral for full-time role
2. **Internship cohort** → See where they landed, get referrals
3. **HR/recruiting contact** → Express interest in full-time conversion

#### UMD Network
1. **QUEST alumni** → LinkedIn: "University of Maryland" + "QUEST" + [Target Company]
2. **Career Center counselors** → Schedule appointments, get introductions
3. **Professors** → Especially capstone advisor for industry connections
4. **Engineering department** → Alumni events, corporate info sessions

#### Greek Network
- Sigma Phi Epsilon alumni in engineering/target industries
- National fraternity career resources

### Cold Outreach Template (LinkedIn)
```
Subject: UMD QUEST Student - Question About [Company]

Hi [Name],

I'm a senior in UMD's QUEST Honors Program studying mechanical engineering, 
graduating in May. I noticed you're a [Title] at [Company] and would love 
to learn more about your experience there.

I'm particularly interested in [specific area] given my background in 
[relevant experience]. Would you have 15 minutes for a quick call or coffee chat?

Thanks for considering,
Carter
```

### Coffee Chat Questions
1. "What's a typical day like in your role?"
2. "What skills are most important for success at [Company]?"
3. "What do you wish you'd known when you started?"
4. "What's the interview process like?"
5. "Is there anyone else you'd recommend I speak with?"
6. "Would you be open to referring me when I apply?"

### Networking Events
- UMD Engineering Career Fair (check dates)
- ASME regional events
- SAE Collegiate Design competitions
- Defense industry career fairs
- Virtual info sessions hosted by target companies

### The 2-Week Rule
After any networking conversation:
- Send thank-you email within 24 hours
- Connect on LinkedIn with personalized note
- Follow up in 2 weeks with relevant article/update
- Update them when you apply to their company

## Job Boards & Resources

### Primary Job Boards
| Platform | Best For | Notes |
|----------|----------|-------|
| **LinkedIn Jobs** | All industries | Set alerts for target titles, use "Easy Apply" strategically |
| **Indeed** | Volume search | Good for regional search, varies in quality |
| **Handshake** | Campus recruiting | UMD-specific postings, NCG programs |
| **Company Career Sites** | Direct applications | Often required even if found elsewhere |

### Specialized Job Boards
| Platform | Focus |
|----------|-------|
| **ClearanceJobs.com** | Defense/security clearance roles |
| **USAJobs.gov** | Federal government (Picatinny, etc.) |
| **EV.Careers** | Electric vehicle industry |
| **EngineerJobs.com** | Engineering-specific |
| **ASME Career Center** | Mechanical engineering |

### Company New Grad Programs (Direct Links)
- **BAE Systems**: jobs.baesystems.com/global/en/early-career
- **Lockheed Martin**: lockheedmartin.com/careers/students-early-careers
- **Northrop Grumman**: jobs.northropgrumman.com
- **Toyota**: careers.toyota.com → Search "New Graduate"
- **Honda**: careers.honda.com
- **Tesla**: tesla.com/careers → University section
- **Accenture**: accenture.com/careers → Entry Level
