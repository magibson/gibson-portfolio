## Interview Preparation

### Common Technical Questions for ME Interviews

**Design & Analysis:**
- "Walk me through your capstone project."
- "How would you approach designing [X product]?"
- "Explain your FEA process and how you validate results."
- "What is DFMEA and how have you used it?"
- "Describe a time you had to troubleshoot a design problem."

**Fundamentals (Brush up on):**
- Stress/strain relationships
- Heat transfer basics
- Fluid mechanics fundamentals
- Materials selection criteria
- Manufacturing processes (machining, casting, 3D printing)
- GD&T basics

**Software:**
- "Walk me through creating [assembly/drawing] in SolidWorks"
- "How do you set up an FEA simulation?"
- "What experience do you have with MATLAB/Python?"

### Behavioral Questions (STAR Method)

**Leadership:**
- "Tell me about a time you led a team through a difficult project."
- "Describe a conflict you resolved on a team."
- → Use Snider Consulting, QUEST projects, fraternity leadership

**Problem-Solving:**
- "Tell me about a technical challenge you overcame."
- "Describe a time you had to learn something quickly."
- → Use capstone project, BAE internship

**Teamwork:**
- "How do you handle disagreements with team members?"
- "Tell me about a successful collaboration."
- → Use QUEST interdisciplinary projects

### Questions to Ask Interviewers
1. "What does success look like in the first 6 months?"
2. "What projects would I be working on initially?"
3. "How is the team structured?"
4. "What's the mentorship/training like for new engineers?"
5. "What do you enjoy most about working here?"
6. "What are the biggest challenges facing the team right now?"

### Interview Day Tips
- Research the interviewer(s) on LinkedIn
- Prepare 3-5 company-specific questions
- Bring printed resume copies
- Prepare capstone project summary (2-minute version)
- Have specific examples ready for behavioral questions
- Send thank-you emails within 24 hours
