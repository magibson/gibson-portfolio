---

### 🏠 LODGING (~$150-300/person for 3 nights)

**Option A: Large Airbnb in Condado (RECOMMENDED)**
- 10-person house/condo: ~$400-600/night = $1,200-1,800 total
- **Per person: $120-180 for 3 nights**
- [Search Airbnb Condado](https://www.airbnb.com/s/Condado--San-Juan--Puerto-Rico/homes?adults=10)

**Option B: Two Airbnbs (more space)**
- 2x 5-person units: ~$200-300/night each = $1,200-1,800 total
- **Per person: $120-180 for 3 nights**

**Option C: Hotel (La Concha, Condado Vanderbilt)**
- ~$250-400/night per room, need 5 rooms
- **Per person: $250-400 for 3 nights**
- [Search Hotels](https://www.booking.com/searchresults.html?ss=Condado%2C+San+Juan)

**Best areas to stay:**
- **Condado** - Beach + restaurants + bars (walkable)
- **Isla Verde** - More resort-y, near airport
- **Old San Juan** - Historic, great bars, no beach

# 🏆 FINAL RECOMMENDATION

**If most of the group is budget-conscious:** Puerto Rico ($700-1,000)
- Best value, no passport, beach + party + adventure

**If you can stretch to $1,200-1,500:** Costa Rica
- More adventure activities, still has nightlife

**If everyone's willing to spend $1,500-2,000:** Iceland
- Once-in-a-lifetime, completely different from Vegas/Nashville

**If you want cheap & easy:** Austin ($600-800)
- Save money for another trip, still a blast
