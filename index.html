About Me

Hi, I'm Matthew Gibson.

I'm a New Jersey native who's been obsessed with cameras since I was old enough to sneak my parents' point-and-shoot at every birthday party or holiday. What started as "borrowing" their gear eventually turned into a hobby I can't imagine living without.

Every spare moment is spent taking pictures. Whether it's chasing light along the Jersey Shore, a vacation to somewhere like Italy, or just finding cool shots in my own backyard, I'm always looking for that one frame that makes you stop scrolling.

I don't travel often, but when I do get away, or even when I'm just exploring close to home, I bring the camera and the drone and try to come back with photos worth sharing.

Thanks for stopping by.
