# Guys Trip Research - 10 People, 3 Nights, ~$2k Budget

**Group:** ~10 guys, mid-20s, from Philly/NYC/Maryland/Michigan  
**Past trips:** Vegas, Nashville  
**Vibe:** Nightlife + daytime adventures (50/50)  
**Budget:** ~$2k/person all-in (flexible)

---

## 🇮🇸 ICELAND (Reykjavik)

### The Vibe
Epic landscapes, Northern Lights, geysers, waterfalls, glaciers. Reykjavik has surprisingly good nightlife. This is the "bucket list" pick.

### Costs (per person, 3 nights)
| Category | Budget | Mid-Range |
|----------|--------|-----------|
| Flights (from NYC) | $370-450 RT | $370-450 RT |
| Accommodation | $120-180 (hostel/Airbnb split) | $300-400 (hotel split) |
| Food & Drinks | $150-200 (mix cooking/eating out) | $250-350 |
| Activities | $100-200 (Golden Circle, Blue Lagoon) | $300-500 |
| **TOTAL** | **$740-1,030** | **$1,220-1,700** |

### Pros
- Once-in-a-lifetime landscapes
- Northern Lights (Sept-March)
- Blue Lagoon + glacier hikes + waterfalls
- Reykjavik nightlife is legit (bars open until 4am weekends)
- Bragging rights

### Cons
- EXPENSIVE (beer = $12-15, meals = $30-50)
- Weather unpredictable
- Nightlife only in Reykjavik
- Need to rent cars to see anything outside city (~$100-150/day for group)

### Best Time
- Northern Lights: Sept-March
- Midnight Sun: June-July
- Cheapest: Shoulder seasons (May, Sept-Oct)

### Sample Itinerary
- **Day 1:** Arrive, Blue Lagoon, Reykjavik bar crawl
- **Day 2:** Golden Circle (Geysir, Gullfoss, Thingvellir) - group tour or rent SUV
- **Day 3:** Glacier hike or snorkeling Silfra, last night out
- **Day 4:** Fly home

## 🇵🇷 PUERTO RICO (San Juan)

### The Vibe
Beach + nightlife + adventure without a passport. La Placita parties go until sunrise. El Yunque rainforest is 45 min away. Best bang-for-buck option.

### Costs (per person, 3 nights)
| Category | Budget | Mid-Range |
|----------|--------|-----------|
| Flights (from PHL/NYC) | $100-200 RT | $150-250 RT |
| Accommodation | $150-200 (Airbnb split in Condado) | $250-350 (hotel) |
| Food & Drinks | $120-180 | $200-300 |
| Activities | $50-150 | $150-300 |
| **TOTAL** | **$420-730** | **$750-1,200** |

### Pros
- **NO PASSPORT NEEDED** (it's a US territory)
- Cheap flights (often under $150 RT)
- Legendary nightlife (La Placita, Condado, Old San Juan)
- Beach + rainforest + history all accessible
- Great food scene (mofongo, lechón)
- US dollars, US cell service

### Cons
- Hurricane season (June-Nov) - risky
- Can feel touristy in peak season
- Less "exotic" than international

### Nightlife Hotspots
- **La Placita (Santurce):** THE spot - outdoor party plaza, goes all night
- **Condado:** Beach bars, rooftop lounges
- **Old San Juan:** La Factoria (famous cocktail bar), cobblestone bar hopping

### Sample Itinerary
- **Day 1:** Arrive, beach in Condado, La Placita at night
- **Day 2:** El Yunque rainforest hike + waterfalls, Old San Juan dinner/bars
- **Day 3:** Bioluminescent bay tour (Fajardo) or beach day, final night out
- **Day 4:** Fly home

## 🇨🇷 COSTA RICA (Tamarindo/Guanacaste)

### The Vibe
"Party and Play" - surf, zip-line, ATV by day, beach bars and clubs at night. Adventure capital meets chill beach town.

### Costs (per person, 3 nights)
| Category | Budget | Mid-Range |
|----------|--------|-----------|
| Flights (from NYC) | $280-400 RT | $350-500 RT |
| Accommodation | $100-150 (hostel/Airbnb) | $200-300 (resort split) |
| Food & Drinks | $80-120 | $150-250 |
| Activities | $100-200 | $250-400 |
| **TOTAL** | **$560-870** | **$950-1,450** |

### Pros
- Perfect adventure/party balance
- Zip-lining, ATV, surfing, fishing
- Tamarindo has solid nightlife
- Beautiful beaches
- More affordable than Iceland

### Cons
- Passport required
- 5+ hour flight
- Need transportation between activities
- Rainy season (May-Nov)

### Activities
- Surf lessons
- Zip-line canopy tours
- ATV jungle tours
- Deep sea fishing
- Volcano hikes (day trip to Arenal)

## 🤠 AUSTIN, TEXAS (Wild Card Domestic)

### The Vibe
Live music capital, legendary 6th Street nightlife, BBQ, Lake Travis. Easy domestic trip that still feels like an adventure.

### Costs (per person, 3 nights)
| Category | Budget | Mid-Range |
|----------|--------|-----------|
| Flights | $150-250 RT | $200-350 RT |
| Accommodation | $100-150 (Airbnb split) | $200-300 |
| Food & Drinks | $100-150 | $200-300 |
| Activities | $50-100 | $100-200 |
| **TOTAL** | **$400-650** | **$700-1,150** |

### Pros
- CHEAP - could do this for $600-800/person easily
- 6th Street is wild (100+ bars in one area)
- Amazing BBQ and Tex-Mex
- Lake Travis boat rentals
- Live music everywhere
- Easy flights from all your cities

### Cons
- Less "exotic" - similar vibe to Nashville
- Hot AF in summer
- No beach

### Daytime Activities
- Lake Travis boat/party barge rental
- BBQ crawl (Franklin, Terry Black's, la Barbecue)
- Top Golf
- Brewery tours (Austin Beerworks, Jester King)

## 🏆 MY RECOMMENDATIONS

### If budget is tight → **PUERTO RICO**
Best value by far. Cheap flights, no passport, beach + nightlife + adventure. Under $1k/person easy.

### If you want unforgettable → **ICELAND**
More expensive but absolutely epic. Nothing like standing on a glacier or seeing the Northern Lights with the boys. Budget $1,500-2,000 to do it right.

### If you want adventure + party balance → **COSTA RICA**
Surf, zip-line, ATV during the day, beach bars at night. Middle ground between PR and Iceland.

### If you want easy & cheap → **AUSTIN**
Save money for another trip. 6th Street is legitimately wild, great food, Lake Travis is fun. Under $700/person.
