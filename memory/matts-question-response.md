## Information Gaps (by goal)

### 1. 💼 Grow Your Book of Business

**What would help:**
- What does your ideal client look like? (Age, income, life stage, concerns)
- What's your current prospecting routine? (Calls/week, meetings/week, sources)
- What part of the sales process do you struggle with most? (Finding leads? Setting appointments? Closing?)
- Are there activity metrics you're supposed to hit? (NYL quotas?)
- What's your follow-up system like? (Do leads slip through cracks?)
- What does your manager say you need to improve?

**Why it helps:** I could build prospecting tools tailored to YOUR ideal client, help track activity, automate follow-ups, and identify patterns in what's working.
