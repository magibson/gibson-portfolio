# Welcome to Your Second Brain

This is your personal notes vault. Jarvis reads everything you write here.

## How to use it
- Write notes freely — shoots you're planning, lead call notes, project ideas, anything
- Jarvis picks up changes within seconds and uses them as context
- Think of it as a shared workspace between you and your AI

## Suggested folders
- 📷 Photography/ — shoot ideas, location notes, gear thoughts
- 💼 Leads/ — call notes, follow-up reminders, prospect research
- 🏗️ Projects/ — ideas, plans, business concepts
- 📓 Journal/ — anything else on your mind

## Tips
- Write naturally, don't overthink structure
- Even rough notes help — "call Julius back in 2 weeks" is enough
- Jarvis will resurface things when they're relevant
