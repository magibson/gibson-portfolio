Quick Reference Panel
Mortgage Protection Benefits
  • Keep the family in the home if income is disrupted.
  • Coverage that tracks the mortgage balance.
  • Affordable monthly payments compared to term options.
  • Fast claims support to avoid missed payments.
Common Objections & Responses
  • "I already have coverage." → "Great — is it designed to pay the mortgage directly?"
  • "Not interested." → "Totally fair — quick question: would your family be okay with the payment if something happened?"
  • "Too expensive." → "Most plans are less than one utility bill — want a quick range?"
  • "Call me later." → "Happy to — what day is better, Tuesday or Thursday?"
Closing Phrases
  • "Let’s get you a quick quote so you can decide."
  • "If the payment is comfortable, do you want to lock it in?"
  • "Would you prefer a 15-year or 30-year style plan?"
  • "I can email the details — what’s the best address?"
