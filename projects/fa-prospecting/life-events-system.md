### 🏠 REAL ESTATE / PROPERTY RECORDS

**Monmouth County Clerk - Property Records (FREE)**
- **URL:** https://oprs.co.monmouth.nj.us/oprs/clerk/clerkhome.aspx?op=basic
- **What it shows:** Deed transfers, new mortgages, property sales
- **Record Room:** 33 Mechanic Street, Freehold, NJ 07728

**Monmouth County Sales Data:**
- https://oprs.co.monmouth.nj.us/oprs/External.aspx?iId=13

**Other NJ Counties:**
Each county has similar OPRS (Open Public Records Search) systems:
- Search "[County Name] NJ OPRS" or "[County Name] NJ property records"

**What to look for:**
- First-time buyers (smaller mortgage amounts, no prior ownership)
- Young couples purchasing together
- Upgrading families (larger home purchases)

### 🏢 NEW BUSINESS FILINGS

**NJ Division of Revenue - Business Entity Search (FREE)**
- **URL:** https://www.njportal.com/dor/businessnamesearch/
- **Business Records Service:** https://www.njportal.com/dor/businessrecords/

**What to search:**
- New LLC formations in Monmouth County
- New corporations filed in past 30 days
- Trade name registrations

**Tip:** Filter by filing date (newest first) and principal address in Monmouth County

## 5. COMPLIANCE NOTES

### ✅ COMPLIANT ACTIVITIES (DO)

- **Use only publicly available information**
  - Newspaper announcements (designed to be public)
  - Public property records (government databases)
  - Public business filings
  - LinkedIn profiles (publicly visible)
  - Public Facebook posts (not private/friends-only)
  - Chamber of Commerce announcements

- **Legitimate outreach methods**
  - Personal introductions through mutual connections
  - Attending public networking events
  - Congratulatory notes without sales pitch
  - Educational seminars/workshops
  - Asking for referrals from existing clients

- **Document your sources**
  - Keep record of where you found each name
  - Screenshot public announcements (for your records)
  - Note the date information was publicly available

### ❌ NON-COMPLIANT ACTIVITIES (DO NOT)

- **Never access private/restricted data**
  - No purchasing lead lists
  - No accessing databases requiring unauthorized credentials
  - No scraping websites that prohibit it
  - No accessing private social media content

- **Never misrepresent yourself**
  - Don't create fake profiles to access private groups
  - Don't claim to be someone you're not
  - Always identify yourself as a financial advisor when asked

- **Respect privacy**
  - Don't collect information about minors
  - Don't share collected information with others
  - Don't persist if someone declines contact
  - Honor do-not-contact requests immediately

### 📋 NEW YORK LIFE COMPLIANCE

- Follow all New York Life policies on prospecting
- Use only approved communication materials
- Document all contacts in approved CRM
- Consult your compliance officer if uncertain
- All advertising/marketing must be pre-approved

### HIPAA Note
- Never seek or use health information
- Medical information is strictly off-limits
- Even if someone posts publicly about health, don't use it for targeting

## 6. OUTREACH BEST PRACTICES

### Warm Introduction Strategies

1. **The Congratulatory Note** (No sell)
   - Send a simple congratulations card
   - No business pitch
   - Just genuine well-wishes
   - Include your card "in case you ever need..."

2. **The Educational Offer**
   - "I put together a checklist for new homeowners..."
   - "Here's a guide for new parents on financial planning..."
   - Value-first approach

3. **The Mutual Connection**
   - "Our mutual friend [name] mentioned you just..."
   - Referrals are always warmest

4. **The Community Member**
   - Meet them at community events
   - Be a genuine participant, not just prospecting
   - Build relationship before business

### Timing Guidelines

| Life Event | Best Outreach Window |
|------------|---------------------|
| New Home | 30-60 days after closing |
| Engagement | 3-6 months before wedding |
| New Baby | 1-3 months after birth |
| Job Change | 60-90 days (after settled) |
| New Business | 3-6 months (after launched) |
| Retirement | 6-12 months before if known |
