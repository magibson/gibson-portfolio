## 1. 🕐 Time Management Systems

### Pomodoro Technique
- **What:** 25-minute focused work blocks + 5-minute breaks
- **Why it's trending:** Simple, proven, works well with digital timers
- **Potential fit:** Good for prospecting calls (batch 4-5 in a Pomodoro session)

### Time Blocking (Cal Newport style)
- **What:** Pre-schedule every hour of your day, including "deep work" blocks
- **Why it works:** Forces intentionality, eliminates decision fatigue
- **Potential fit:** Block mornings for calls/appointments, afternoons for admin

### The Eisenhower Matrix
- **What:** Categorize tasks as Urgent/Important, Important/Not Urgent, etc.
- **Why it matters:** Helps prioritize what actually moves the needle
- **Potential fit:** Your "lever" is appointments—that's Quadrant 1

## 3. 📚 Knowledge Management (Second Brain)

### PARA Method (Tiago Forte)
Organize everything into 4 categories:
- **P**rojects: Active goals with deadlines
- **A**reas: Ongoing responsibilities (health, finances, career)
- **R**esources: Topics of interest (photography tips, investment research)
- **A**rchives: Completed/inactive items

**Why it's relevant:** You've got years of photography backlog, business materials, health goals—a system could help resurface the right info at the right time.

### Tools for Second Brain
- Notion (what I currently use for you)
- Obsidian (local, markdown-based)
- Capacities (newer, object-oriented)

## 4. 🎯 Deep Work & Focus

### Cal Newport's Framework
1. **Schedule deep work** (2-4 hour blocks, no interruptions)
2. **Embrace boredom** (don't reach for phone during idle moments)
3. **Quit social media** (or use it intentionally with strict time limits)
4. **Drain the shallows** (minimize meetings, emails, admin)

### Practical Applications for You
- **Deep work block:** 2 hours for prospecting calls without checking anything else
- **Shallow work block:** Batch emails, paperwork, follow-ups
- **Productive meditation:** Use commute/gym time to think through one problem

### Focus Tools
- **Focus apps:** Freedom, Cold Turkey, one sec (delay before opening distracting apps)
- **Website blockers:** Block Twitter/Instagram during work hours
- **Grayscale mode:** Makes phone less appealing

## 6. 🏃 Habit & Routine Building

### Atomic Habits Approach (James Clear)
- **Habit stacking:** Attach new habits to existing ones
- **2-minute rule:** Start with tiny versions of habits
- **Environment design:** Make good habits easy, bad habits hard

### Morning Routine Optimization
Many productivity systems emphasize a "power hour" or "miracle morning":
- Wake time consistency
- No phone first 30 min
- Exercise/movement
- One high-priority task before email

### Potential for You
- Stack gym with prospecting prep (listen to sales training while working out)
- Environment design: Phone charges outside bedroom
- 2-minute rule: "I'll just make one prospecting call" (often leads to more)
