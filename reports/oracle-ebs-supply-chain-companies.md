## Key Findings

### Industry Distribution of Oracle EBS Users
- **Manufacturing** - Highest concentration
- **Distribution/Logistics** - Strong presence
- **Pharmaceuticals** - Several major players
- **Medical Devices** - Notable adopters
- **Aerospace & Defense** - Significant use case

### Oracle EBS SCM Modules Commonly Used
- Inventory Management
- Order Management
- Warehouse Management (WMS)
- Transportation Management (OTM)
- Advanced Supply Chain Planning (ASCP)
- Procurement

### Notes
1. **DHL Supply Chain** was previously on Oracle EBS but has migrated to Oracle Fusion Cloud ERP (as of 2020)
2. **FedEx** is in the process of transitioning from legacy systems to Oracle Cloud ERP/SCM
3. Many companies are on hybrid implementations (EBS on-prem + Oracle Cloud components)
